FiveClues Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how FiveClues ("the App") collects, uses, and stores information.

Information We Collect

The App may collect and store the following information:

  • Game progress, unlocked content, coins, hints, and other gameplay-related data
  • Sign in with Apple identifier, only when you choose to sign in for backup and restore
  • Purchase and entitlement information related to in-app purchases
  • Basic technical data needed for app functionality, security, and service operation

How We Use Information

  • To operate the game and save your progress
  • To let you back up and restore data using Sign in with Apple
  • To process and restore in-app purchase entitlements
  • To improve app stability, functionality, and user experience

Advertising

The App may display ads using third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob. These services may process device-related information to serve and measure ads in accordance with their own policies.

The App does not require you to create an account to view ads. If tracking permission is not requested or granted, ads may still be shown in a non-personalized manner where supported.

Sign in with Apple

If you choose to use Sign in with Apple, the App uses your Apple account only for backup and restore of your game data. This feature is optional.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information may be processed by service providers that support the App's functionality, such as cloud storage, authentication, in-app purchases, and advertising services.

Data Retention

Game-related data may be retained as long as necessary to provide backup, restore, purchases, and core app functionality.

Your Choices

  • You may choose not to use Sign in with Apple
  • You may delete the App from your device at any time
  • You may use available platform settings to manage permissions and purchases

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended to collect personal information from children knowingly beyond what is required for app functionality.
